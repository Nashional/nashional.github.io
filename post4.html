The Klaw.

The San-Antonio Spurs have been the model for consistency in the NBA. Having made the playoffs for the past 18 seasons, the Spurs continue to be a powerhouse in the West.

Can the man with Goliath-sized hands claim his second NBA crown>

Around this time last year, Kawhi Leonard was in the discussion for best player in the NBA.

An injury plagued season has everyone forgetting about the Spurs, but if Kawhi can come back and allow the team to gain some momentum the Spurs can easily be a top contender.

Reaching for the Crown

Champions do not become champions when they win the event, but in the hours, weeks, months nad years they spend preparing for it. The victorious performance itself is merely the demonstration of their championship character. - T. Alan Armstrong

So will Kawhi come back and help lead the Spurs to a 6th championship? Or will arguably the most rounded team in the league, helmed by undoubtedly the best coach come up short?
